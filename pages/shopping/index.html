Key Features for Smart Procurement

Automate the tedious process of ordering and inventory management directly from your finalized menus and recipes.

Auto-Generate from Recipes

Pulls scaled ingredients directly

Select multiple scaled recipes to combine their total ingredient needs.

Ingredients are aggregated by type and total quantity required.

Ensures accurate bulk ordering quantities for minimal waste.

Procurement Benefit Bulk Precision

Categorized Shopping

Auto-sorts by store section

Items are automatically sorted into **Produce**, **Proteins & Dairy**, and **Pantry**.

Custom categories can be added and managed via the Settings tab.

Enables rapid, efficient movement through your market or supplier list.

Procurement Benefit Speed & Flow

Real-Time Cost Tracking

Calculates subtotal and managed spend

Enter prices per item to calculate a live running **Subtotal** for your order.

Price tracking excludes items you have marked as checked/purchased.

Track spending for specific menus and production runs.

Procurement Benefit Budget Control

Actionable Purchasing

Check, clear, and manage items

Easily mark items as purchased by toggling a simple checkbox.

Use the "Clear Checked" button to quickly remove all purchased items.

Add custom items instantly with quantity and optional price via modal.

Procurement Benefit Workflow Clarity

Export & Share Lists

Print or share with team/suppliers

Export your fully categorized and finalized shopping list as a professional PDF or TXT file.

Use the PDF format for clean, professional communication with suppliers.

The TXT file is perfect for easy copy-pasting into ordering systems or emails.

Procurement Benefit Supplier Ready

Market Management

Organize your suppliers

Save and manage all your supplier, market, and purveyor contact information.

Store addresses, phone numbers, and specific ordering notes for each market.

Keeps procurement contacts separate but easily accessible alongside your lists.
