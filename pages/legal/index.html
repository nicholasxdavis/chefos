2. Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how ChefOS by Blacnova ("Company") handles and protects user information collected through the ChefOS Professional Edition application ("Service").

2.1. The Principle of Local Data Storage

ChefOS is fundamentally a local-first application.

  • **Core User Data:** All recipes, menus, shopping lists, and custom settings (like calculator densities or theme) are stored locally on your device's browser local storage. **The Company does NOT transmit or store this data on its servers.**
  • **Authentication Data:** We store only your email address and subscription plan (e.g., 'trial' or 'pro') for the sole purpose of verifying your application access and managing your subscription.
  • **PIN Protection:** Your access PIN is stored securely (hashed) on your local device for data protection features and is **never transmitted** to the Company.

2.2. Information We Collect (Only for Service Provision)

  • **Registration Information:** Email address and encrypted password (or PIN) for authentication.
  • **Billing/Subscription Status:** Data necessary to track your plan (trial, pro) and expiry date, stored locally and potentially on a secure, third-party payment processor (not disclosed to the Company).
  • **Device/Usage Data (Limited):** Basic non-identifying technical data (e.g., screen resolution, browser type) to ensure application compatibility and performance. We do not use third-party analytics trackers.

2.3. Data Storage and Transfers (Protecting You)

  • **No Sale of Data:** We do not sell, rent, or share any of your personal data or User Data to third parties for monetary consideration or cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • **Cloud Sync (Optional):** If you choose to enable the optional Cloud Sync feature, you are electing to transfer a copy of your User Data (recipes, menus, lists, etc.) to the Company's servers for the sole purpose of enabling multi-device access and backup. This data remains confidential and is encrypted both in transit and at rest.
  • **PDF Management:** Files uploaded to the PDF Management feature are encoded and stored locally by default. If Cloud Sync is enabled, these files will be backed up to the Company's servers.
