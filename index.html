The Professional Kitchen Toolkit

Everything you need to run an efficient, profitable kitchen with absolute consistency.

Recipe Scaler

Scale any recipe from 1 portion to 1000. Our professional scaling engine handles weights, volumes, and units flawlessly, saving you time and preventing errors.

Menu Management

Build, manage, and cost your menus. Combine saved recipes into complete service menus and store your PDF files, all in one organized hub.

Shopping Lists

Generate shopping lists from your menus or recipes. Manage suppliers, track prices, and calculate subtotals to keep your food costs in check.

Kitchen Calculator

A powerful calculator built for chefs. Convert units (grams to oz, cups to tbsp), calculate ingredient densities, and perform quick kitchen math without leaving the app.

My Recipes

Store and organize all your recipes in one centralized location. Access your complete recipe library anytime, anywhere with full search and filtering capabilities.

My Menus

Create and manage service menus by combining your saved recipes. Plan events, daily specials, and seasonal offerings with complete cost tracking.

PDF Management

Attach and organize PDF menus, recipes, and documents. Keep all your important files linked to menus and recipes for easy reference and sharing.

NEW

My Calendar

Plan your kitchen operations with an integrated calendar. Schedule menu rotations, prep days, and special events to stay organized and efficient.
